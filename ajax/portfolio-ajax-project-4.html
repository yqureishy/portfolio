Project Info:

FruVe Flow is a proof-of-concept/MVP e-commerce site for the nascent grocery delivery business of the family of one of our dev team members.

Project Details:

  • Created By:Nicole Aldurien, Neil Shah, Wesley Kolar and Younus Qureishy
  • Technologies:MERN stack (MongoDB, Express, React, Node), Redux, SendGrid SMTP relay, Javascript, HTML/CSS, Heroku
  • Date:May 2021
